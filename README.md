## About

- Integrated MS/PhD student in Material Engineering with AI at Sejong University, Seoul.
- Researching neuromorphic computing, memristor device simulation, and hardware-aware deep learning.
- Building tools that connect measured LTP/LTD device behavior with neural-network training, quantization, and 3D visualization.

## Research Focus

- Memristive devices for neuromorphic AI hardware
- Device-aware and hardware-constrained neural networks
- In-memory computing and crossbar-array simulation
- Semiconductor stack visualization and interactive scientific tools
- PyTorch training workflows for ideal vs memristor-aware evaluation
